## Overview

**Prompt Symphony** is an open-source platform for AI safety and prompt engineering developed during a Microsoft-organized hackathon. The project addresses critical challenges in AI deployment including prompt validation, jailbreak detection, and responsible AI development practices.

**Live Platform**: [github.com/jazadev/prompt-symphony](https://github.com/jazadev/prompt-symphony)

---

## Challenge Context

**Microsoft Hackathon** focused on responsible AI development and safety. Our team collaborated to create a comprehensive solution for prompt engineering challenges, competing against teams from across the globe in an intense 48-hour development cycle.

**Core Problem**: The rapid adoption of AI technologies requires robust safety measures to prevent harmful outputs while maintaining useful functionality.
