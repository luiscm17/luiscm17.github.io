## Overview

**Retrievbit** is a customized fork of the Astrogon template, adapted specifically for petroleum engineering education. I transformed a general-purpose template into a specialized multilingual platform with structured training programs and technical content.

---

## Project Description

**Key adaptations made to the original Astrogon template:**

- **Domain Specialization**: Focused on petroleum engineering disciplines
- **Multilingual Architecture**: Added English/Spanish content system
- **Educational Structure**: Training programs, technical articles, blog posts
- **Interactive Features**: Search, dark mode, responsive design
- **Performance Optimization**: Static generation with Astro

**Live Platform**: [retrievbit.pages.dev](https://retrievbit.pages.dev)
